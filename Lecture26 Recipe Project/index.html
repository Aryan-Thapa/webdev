Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup of milk powder with 1/4 cup of ghee, then add a few tablespoons of milk to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls, deep fry until golden brown, and soak them in sugar syrup made with 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water.
  3. Garnish with chopped nuts, and your delicious gulab jamuns are ready to be served.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Maida
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. In a pan, combine 2 cups of grated khoya (reduced milk), 1 cup of sugar, and cook on low heat until the mixture thickens.
  2. Add cardamom powder and your choice of chopped nuts, then spread the mixture onto a greased tray, smoothing the top.
  3. Allow it to set, cut into squares, and garnish with more nuts for a delightful homemade barfi.
